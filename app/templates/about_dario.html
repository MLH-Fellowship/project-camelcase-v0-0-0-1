About Dario

Hello All, my name is Dario. I migrated to the United States right before turning 12. I did not speak English when I first arrived in the US, but neither was I able to write or read in Spanish.

Upon arriving in the US. I requested to be placed in an all-English school because I wanted to be able to communicate with other kids. After learning some English, I had to also start learning to read and write in Spanish to be able to communicate with people at home.

I became curious about computers and programming and started doing independent studies into them. I began with HTML, then moved to CSS, and finally JavaScript. In my senior year of high school, my school introduced an AP-CS class, my first formal computer science class.

In college, I was introduced to formal computational theories and more programming languages such as C++, Python, and SQL, and mathematical ways to analyze algorithms.
